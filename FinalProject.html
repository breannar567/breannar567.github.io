Save the Elephants

Elephants are rapidly disappearing from their habitats, this being because poachers will
stop at nothing to obtain ivory from elephant tusks. Even though most areas have
outlawed the poaching of elephants for their tusks, poachers continue to hunt them.
Elephants will become extinct if we don't do something about this soon.

Across Africa, elephants attract tourists that bring in the funding responsible for
protecting wildlife areas. Elephants are very important to their environment because
when they eat, they creat gaps in vegetation, allowing new plants to grow for smaller
animals to eat.

What you can do to Help

There are countless organizations that accept donations, such as WildAid, International Elephant Foundation, and Ivoryella. What does your money do to help elephants?
A number of things. Pays for advertising to raise awareness about the danger elephants
are in, pays for lawyers in elephant protection cases, even pays to build better elephant
reservations.
